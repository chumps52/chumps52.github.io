Katz Responses: Table



Select a Patient ID from the dropdown and press "Generate Table" to view the select patient's Katz responses as a table.

  • Dates are in the format "MM-dd-yyyy-HH:mm:ss".
  • Columns are resizeable.
  • Download buttons will appear after table generation.
  • NB: Downloading as a PDF may cause the table to be truncated.
